Who we are

We are a community of student, academic and business leaders committed to using the power of entrepreneurial action to transform lives and shape a better, more sustainable world.

Enactus MH attempts to help improve the situation of underprivileged women who are willing to work but haven't been provided enough opportunities. The main aim of our projects is to provide them a long term and sustainable means of income that would eventually lead to their economic and social upliftment.

About Enactus

Enactus brings together a diverse network of university students, academic professionals and industry leaders around the world with the shared mission of creating a better, more sustainable world through the positive power of business. The Enactus team aims solely at the improvement of the conditions of the ones 'in need'.

With a motto of 'A head for business. A heart for the world.'; Enactus has been successful in changing the lives of millions across borders.

Seeing possibilities. Taking action. Enabling progress.
